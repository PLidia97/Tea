How to make tea

At the very least, tea is flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant - based (Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's niceley aereted, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug (always a big mug)
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs to unlock all its flavour, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its thing. This id perfect time to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about vacation.
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon agains the side of the mug. Not to much or you'll make it bitter.
  6. If you want, throe in some milk or sugar or honey or nothin at all.
  7. Enjoy!
